Applied Mathematics

The main goal of the subject is to improve a student’s mathematical, computational, and communication skills. The focus is on modeling problems from a variety of areas including business, engineering and science.

What will we learn?

  • Differential Equations
  • Qualitative Analysis of ODEs
  • The Laplace Transform
  • The Finite Fourier Transform
  • Properties of the Fourier Transform
  • Vectors

Requirements

To join this course without any understanding problem, you are required to have a basic knowledge on linear algebra, statistics, single and multivariable calculus.
