History

This history subject, is about the history of the world, starting from the origins of the human race, drawing together the great themes of civilisation with details from everyday life and it ends in the present with the evolution of the developed civilisations.

What will we learn?

  • Homo Sapiens
  • Civilisations
  • The Classical Age
  • The Age of Diverging Traditions
  • The Making of European Age
  • The Great Acceleration
  • The End of the European Age
  • Our Own Time: The Cold War World and the Crises

Requirements

There are not academical requirements to study World History, but a good knowledge of geography will make this subject easier and more readable.
