Statistics

The main goal of this subject is to use a limited sample to make intelligent and accurate conclusions about a greater population. The use of tables, graphs and charts play a vital role in presenting the data being used to draw these conclusions.

What will we learn?

  • Probability Model
  • Random Variables and Distributions
  • Expectation
  • Sampling Distributions and Limits
  • Likelihood Inference
  • Bayesian Inference
  • Optimal Inferences
  • Model Checking
  • Advanced Topic — Stochastic Processes

Requirements

If you plan to attend the lesson of statistics, you will be required to have a strong background in mathematics. Specifically, the two topics that you need the most are calculus and linear algebra.
