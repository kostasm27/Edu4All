Introduction to Algorithm Design

The main goal of the subject is to give a proper introduction on basic Algorith theorems , focusing on Complexity and Efficiency. We will focus on algorithmic techiques , decision algorithms and solutions on some of the most important NP problems , giving extra weigth on solving diffent tasks and exercises. We will be focusing on analyzing and developing , so a basic knowledge on data structures is required.

What will we learn?

  • Introduction to Algorithm Analysis
  • Complexity and Efficiency , Big O , Ω , Θ
  • Introduction to basic Graph theorems and terms
  • Depth First Search (DFS), Breadth First Search (BFS) , connectivity , DAGs
  • Sorting Algorithms ( Quickshort , Mergeshort , Heapshort) , efficiency and worst cases
  • Divide and Conquer , Decrease and Conquer , Greedy Algorithms, Master Theorem
  • NP problems , Dynamic Programming
  • Optimization

Requirements

A basic knowlegde on data structures is required ( HashMap ,HashSet ,Trees ,MinHeap ,Maxheap etc) , although we'll be focusing on Analysis. A small amount of programming exercises will be provided( on Java) , in order to practice your developing skills( we will be using Intellij as our main API ).
